Privacy Policy & Data Safety

Application: Hijri/Gregorian Date Converter (Date Converter)

Last Updated: May 31, 2026

We deeply value your privacy. This policy outlines how our application interacts with data, ensuring a transparent and secure user experience. By using the app, you consent to the practices described herein.

1. General Information & Identity

  • App Function: A dedicated utility to convert dates between the Hijri and Gregorian calendars, browse Islamic occasions, and receive related localized notifications.
  • No User Accounts: The app operates fully without requiring any user registration, accounts, or personal logins.
  • No Sensitive Data Collection: We strictly do not collect, request, or store personal identifiers such as your full name, email address, phone number, or precise GPS location.
  • Contact: For privacy concerns, reach out to the developer directly at: naram3096@gmail.com.

2. Local Storage & Data Retention

The application stores essential configuration data locally on your device via SharedPreferences to retain user preferences. This data is never uploaded to external servers.

🕒 Data Retention Note: To ensure maximum privacy and optimal device performance, all locally cached technical settings, counters, and temporary event data are scheduled to be automatically deleted after 90 days of inactivity.

3. Third-Party Services & Analytics

We integrate reliable core third-party SDKs to improve application quality, analyze technical stability, and support development through advertisements:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics & Crashlytics): Monitors generalized, aggregated app performance metrics and receives encrypted technical logs following unexpected crashes to facilitate debug fixes.
  • Google AdMob: Serves standard contextual advertisements. Google automatically processes technical attributes such as your device's Advertising ID and IP address. A compliant consent mechanism (UMP SDK) is active to handle user choices regarding ad personalization.
  • AlAdhan API & Wikipedia: Used strictly to fetch calendar parameters and event context without passing or linking personal telemetry.

4. System Permissions

The app requests the following core system permissions for structural functionality only:

  • INTERNET & ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE: Required to establish safe connections for calendar updates and ad fetching.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS, SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM & RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED: Used to correctly time local reminders for Islamic occasions based entirely on your device's local clock configuration, without processing physical tracking coordinates.

Privacy Policy for Calendar Converter App

Version: 2.0.0
Last Updated: May 31, 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how information is handled within the Calendar Converter application (Package Name: com.dbs.dateconverter).

1. General & Identity

  • Developer/Data Controller: naram3096@gmail.com
  • No Accounts: No registration or user login is required.
  • Data We Do NOT Collect: We do not collect names, emails, phone numbers, or GPS location data.

2. On-Device Local Storage

Data is stored strictly locally via SharedPreferences and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Language selection (13 languages + system language).
  • Theme selection (Light / Dark / System default).
  • Cached Islamic occasions calendar database.
  • App open counters and last review request timestamps.

3. Third-Party Services (Android Only)

  • Firebase Analytics: Tracks screen logs (holidays_tab, converter_tab, settings, occasions_browser). Automatically disabled in Debug mode.
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Collects stack traces and hardware parameters to troubleshoot errors.
  • Firebase Remote Config: Remotely fetches the application's active privacy policy URL.

4. Advertisements — Google AdMob

Integrates App Open, Interstitial, Rewarded Interstitial (optional before sharing), and Native ads within the occasions dashboard.

  • Data Collected: Advertising ID, IP address, device type, and OS version.
  • Consent Management: Personalized ad tracking consent is obtained via Google UMP SDK (compliant with GDPR).
  • Google Ads Policy.

5. External Integrations

  • AlAdhan API: Synchronizes Gregorian calendar arrays without processing any personal metrics.
  • Google Fonts: Requests typeface designs (may expose your IP address to Google).
  • Wikipedia WebView: Links display historical facts subject to Wikipedia's standalone privacy policies.
  • Local Utilities: Implements Google Play In-App Reviews, sharing intents, and local clipboard actions.

6. Notifications, Permissions & Offline Execution

  • Local Notifications: Schedules reminders for Ramadan and Eids based on system time zone (not GPS). Requires POST_NOTIFICATIONS.
  • System Permissions: INTERNET · ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE · RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED · SCHEDULE_EXACT_ALARM / USE_EXACT_ALARM.
  • Connectivity: Conversion utilities work 100% offline via embedded_occasions.json. Internet is needed only for ads and calendar refreshes.

7. Rights & Legal Compliance

Wiping app storage purges all data instantly. We do not target or harvest data from children under 13. Data is processed globally over Google nodes. For inquiries: naram3096@gmail.com.
